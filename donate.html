How to Support My Campaign

Thank you for considering supporting my fundraising campaign for the public speaking competition in Australia. Your support means a lot to me.

Option 1: BackaBuddy

If you prefer to donate online, you can do so through BackaBuddy, a secure platform for fundraising campaigns. Here's how:

  1. Visit my BackaBuddy fundraising page: BackaBuddy
  2. Click on the "Donate" or "Support Now" button.
  3. Enter the donation amount.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the donation process.

Option 2: Other Means

If you prefer to donate using other methods, such as bank transfers or in-person donations, please visit my contact page

I'll provide you with the necessary information for making a donation outside of BackaBuddy.
